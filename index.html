ABOUT

After graduating in graphic design and art directory, I started working as a web designer in different web creative agency in Milano , where i sharpen my web design and UI/UX skills.
When I moved to Berlin, thanks to its great IT-community that organizes many workshops and events, I dived into web development.

Since relocating to the German capital 10 years ago, I had the chance to grow and work on interesting, on-production applications with lot of traffic in medium-to-big size companies like Civey , DeliveryHero or Rebate Networks . Working with amazing teams, I learnt different workflow techniques like Agile Development and coding best practices.

While I value working in-person, I prefer working remotely, as freelance or contractor.
As remote developer, I had the opportunity to work with amazing companies on the other side of the ocean like Buzzfeed NY and Discourse as well as collaborating with web creative agencies like 100Danish, dEvolute, Artbutler.
Early stage startups like Boatify , Issmeet are my jam. I like environments where architecture and product strategy inputs are very welcome and it is often required to focus on multiple areas of development.

I believe in free education and community support. I co-founded OpenTechSchool where I am also an active organizer. I also support a various open source projects like Nextcloud , Discourse (volunteer) , Wikimedia Foundation .
