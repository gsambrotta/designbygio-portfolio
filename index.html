ABOUT

With a university education in graphic design and art, i began my carrier as web designer in different web design agency in Milano, where i sharpen my design and UI/UX skills.
Once in Berlin, I deepen my knowledge in web development also thanks to the great IT community and the many self-taught workshop and events the city has to offer.

In these almost 10 years i worked with well organize, medium-to-big size companies like Civey, DeliveryHero or Rebate Networks. where i had the chance to work on interesting, on-production applications with lot of traffic. Here i embrace the joy of working with a team, learnt different workflow techniques like Agile Development and coding best practice.

I favour remote work, as freelance or contractor.
These gave me the opportunity to work with amazing company on the other side of the world like Buzzfeed NY and Discourse or collaborate with web agency like 100Danish, dEvolute, Artbutler.
I enjoy a lot working with early stage startup like Boatify, Issmeet where architecture and product strategy input are very welcome and is often required to focus on multiple area of the development.

I believe in free education and community support therefore I co-found OpenTechSchool where i'm still an active organizer. I support a various open source projects like Nextcloud, Discourse (volunteer), Wikimedia Foundation.
